Eligibility for VA disability benefits

The VA disability compensation provides tax-free monthly payments for veterans who have a service-connected condition, you may be eligible!
A service-connected condition means an illness or injury that was caused by—or got worse because of—your active military service.


Keep in mind that the steps below are for eligible veterans only.

Find out your eligibility

Step 1: Gather The Evidence

Prepare medical records, military discharge papers, and any other documents supporting your claim.
We encourage you to send in all your supporting documents along with your claim. This will help your claim being processed quickly.

Step 2: File Online Or By Mail

Submit your claim electronically through VA.gov eBenefits or by mail using the VA Form 21-526EZ.

Mailing the form? Download it here

Need profesional help to file your claim?

Step 3: Attend a C&P Exam (If necessary)

A Compensation and Pension (C&P) exam will be scheduled to assess your disability.

Step 4: Wait For Your Decision

The VA will process your claim and notify you of their decision.
It takes on average 159 days for your claim to be reviewed.
Be patient!

Step 5: Need To Appeal?

If you disagree with the VA's decision, you have the right to appeal.
