Musculoskeletal System

Disability Description Rating
Arthritis Inflammation of joints causing pain and stiffness 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment
Amputation Loss of a limb due to injury or medical condition 10% to 100% depending on number of limbs lost
Back Pain Chronic pain in the back area 10% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment
Fracture Broken bone due to injury or trauma 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment
Osteoporosis Decreased bone density leading to increased fracture risk 0% to 100% depending on severity and complications
Scoliosis Abnormal curvature of the spine 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment
Rheumatoid Arthritis Chronic inflammatory disorder affecting joints 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment

Respiratory System

Disability Description Rating
Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) Lung disease causing breathing difficulties 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment
Asthma Chronic inflammation of the airways leading to breathing difficulty 0% to 100% depending on severity and frequency of attacks
Sleep Apnea Interruption of breathing during sleep 0% to 100% depending on severity and treatment

Cardiovascular System

Disability Description Rating
Ischemic Heart Disease Reduced blood flow to the heart 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment
Hypertension Elevated blood pressure 0% to 100% depending on severity and complications
Peripheral Vascular Disease Restricted blood flow to the limbs 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment
Coronary Artery Disease Blockage of arteries supplying blood to the heart 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment

Neurological System

Disability Description Rating
Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) Psychological condition resulting from a traumatic event 10% to 100% depending on severity
Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI) Brain damage resulting from head injury 0% to 100% depending on severity and cognitive impairment
Migraine Headaches Recurrent headaches accompanied by sensory disturbances 0% to 100% depending on frequency and severity
Epilepsy Neurological disorder characterized by seizures 0% to 100% depending on frequency and severity of seizures

Endocrine System

Disability Description Rating
Diabetes Mellitus Metabolic disorder resulting in high blood sugar levels 0% to 100% depending on complications and severity
Thyroid Disorder Abnormalities in thyroid hormone production 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment
Adrenal Insufficiency Inadequate production of adrenal hormones 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment

Dental and Oral System

Disability Description Rating
Gingivitis Inflammation of the gums characterized by redness, swelling, and bleeding, often caused by poor oral hygiene 0% to 100% depending on complications and severity
Tooth Loss (Edentulism) Complete or partial loss of natural teeth, which can affect chewing ability, speech, and self-esteem 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment
Dental Abscess Collection of pus within the teeth or gums due to bacterial infection, causing severe pain, swelling, and potentially life-threatening complications if left untreated 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment

Digestive System

Disability Description Rating
Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD) Chronic condition where stomach acid flows back into the esophagus, causing irritation and inflammation 0% to 100% depending on complications and severity
Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS) Functional disorder of the gastrointestinal tract characterized by abdominal pain, bloating, and changes in bowel habits without evidence of underlying damage 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment
Celiac Disease Autoimmune disorder triggered by gluten consumption, leading to damage to the small intestine and malabsorption of nutrients 0% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment

The Skin

Disability Description Rating
Dermatitis or Eczema A chronic skin condition characterized by inflammation, itching, and redness of the skin 0% to 100% depending on either palpable or visible disfigurement or upon the additive effect of scars
Psoriasis A condition in which skin cells build up and form scales and itchy, dry patches 0% to 100% depending on either palpable or visible disfigurement or upon the additive effect of scars
Vitiligo A disease that causes the loss of skin color in blotches 0% to 100% depending on either palpable or visible disfigurement or upon the additive effect of scars

Other

Disability Description Rating
Cancer Malignant tumor growth in various parts of the body 0% to 100% depending on type, location, and treatment
Depression Mood disorder characterized by persistent sadness 10% to 100% depending on severity and functional impairment
