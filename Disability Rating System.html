Combined Ratings Table

Combined Ratings Calculator

The VA uses Combined Ratings Table to determine a single disability rating for veterans with multiple disabilities. The calculator below facilitates this process by providing combined values for pairs of disabilities.

The combined value is rounded to the nearest number divisible by 10, adjusting values ending in 5 upward. If there are more than two disabilities, the combined value for the first two is determined and then combined with the third disability, and so forth.
For example, for a veteran with a 60% disability, a 40% disability, and a 20% disability, the VA would first find the combined rating for the 60% and 40% disabilities (76%). Then, they would combine the 76% with the 20% disability to get the final combined rating (80%).
