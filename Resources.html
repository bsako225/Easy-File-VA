Resources for Veterans

Government Agencies and Departments:

  • Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) - Provides a wide range of benefits and services to veterans, including healthcare, disability compensation, education assistance, and home loans.
  • Veterans Health Administration (VHA) - Offers healthcare services to eligible veterans, including medical treatment, mental health services, and rehabilitation programs.
  • Veterans Benefits Administration (VBA) - Administers benefits programs for veterans, such as disability compensation, pension benefits, and vocational rehabilitation.

National Organizations:

  • American Legion - Provides support and advocacy for veterans, including assistance with VA claims, access to healthcare, and community support programs.
  • Veterans of Foreign Wars (VFW) - Offers a range of services for veterans, including financial assistance, advocacy, and support for veterans transitioning to civilian life.
  • Disabled American Veterans (DAV) - Advocates for disabled veterans and provides assistance with VA claims, transportation to medical appointments, and support for veterans' families.

Mental Health and Counseling Services:

  • National Center for PTSD - Offers resources and support for veterans experiencing post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), including information on treatment options and coping strategies.
  • Give an Hour - Provides free mental health counseling services to veterans and their families, connecting them with licensed therapists who volunteer their time.
  • Cohen Veterans Network - Offers low-cost or no-cost mental health services to veterans and their families, including counseling, therapy, and support groups.

Employment and Career Services

  • Hire Heroes USA - Assists veterans with finding employment opportunities, resume writing, interview preparation, and career counseling.
  • Veterans Employment and Training Service (VETS) - Offers resources and support for veterans seeking employment, including job placement assistance, vocational training, and career development services.

Housing and Homelessness Assistance

  • National Coalition for Homeless Veterans (NCHV) - Works to end veteran homelessness by providing resources, support services, and advocacy at the national level.
  • Veterans Village of San Diego (VVSD) - Offers housing and supportive services to homeless and at-risk veterans, including transitional housing, counseling, and job training programs.

Educational Assistance and Scholarships

  • GI Bill - Provides educational benefits to eligible veterans, including funding for tuition, books, and housing expenses.
  • Pat Tillman Foundation - Offers scholarships to veterans and military spouses pursuing higher education, including undergraduate, graduate, and professional degrees.

Legal Assistance

  • Veterans Legal Services - Provides free legal assistance to veterans on issues such as VA benefits claims, discharge upgrades, and civil legal matters.
  • Legal Services Corporation (LSC) - Offers legal aid programs across the United States, providing low-income veterans with access to legal representation and advocacy.
